    Our Story

    Welcome to
    Dr. Dasarathan
    International School!

    Inspire · Explore · Excel

    Students at Dr. DIS are nurtured to be well-educated and disciplined citizens. We accomplish this by providing rich and holistic education with beautiful, sweeping facilities. We ensure our students are engaged appropriately to tackle challenges through joyful learning. We offer a research-based curriculum that emphasises a strong foundation in literacy as well as critical thinking, leadership, and bounty opportunities for creative manifestation.
